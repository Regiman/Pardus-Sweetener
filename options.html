Pardus Sweetener Options

Alarm

The audible alarm can draw your attention back to your computer when important events occur. It may also compel your roommates or family members to appease your computer with a BFH, if you leave it logged into Pardus while you are not around to snooze it.

Desktop notifications

Desktop notifications are subtler than the alarm, and convenient when you are at your computer but doing something else.

Clock

Display time countdowns to several recurring in-game events. For these to be accurate, your computer's time zone should be properly set, and its clock should be synchronised with Coordinated Universal Time. Modern operating systems offer easy means to achieve this; see this article for a quick rundown.
